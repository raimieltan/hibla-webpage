HIBLÁ

A GUILT FREE FASHION EXPERIENCE

Provide Services

A social enterprise that promotes circular fashion through an app that provides services for reimagining or repurposing clothes/fabrics from ordinary consumers which can also be resold through the app.

Provide Jobs

This endeavor can provide jobs or extra income for the local artisans and tailors which are one of our target beneficiaries.

Make better use of it

We can also partner with clothing manufacturers or resellers to give us their excess and unsold clothing in order to make better use of it rather than just dump or burn them which continuously adds to the problem.

Mission

The mission of Hiblá is the dedication to guilt-free products delivered with quality, promoting sustainability, and working hand-in-hand with communities and artisans.

Vision

The vision of Hiblá is to bridge the gap between fashion and sustainability through guilt-free and affordable products that help support local artisans, collaborate with consumers and monolith corporations, and challenge the belief that ethical products translate to expensive products.
